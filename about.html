Our Story

With a passion for education and a deep understanding of student needs, Ava Becker founded Ask Ava to provide personalized tutoring that empowers students. After years of working in various educational settings, she recognized the importance of individualized attention in helping students thrive. Her dedication to fostering confidence and academic excellence drives her mission.

At Ask Ava, our mission is to empower high school students to reach their academic potential through personalized tutoring. We value a supportive learning environment that cultivates confidence, resilience, and a lifelong love for learning.
